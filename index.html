Instructions

The cards in the game come shuffled and face-down.

The player will select a card, click on it and turn it over. The player will then select a second card from the deck, turn it over and see if it's a match. If the cards are a match, the cards will stay up-turned and the player can continue doing the same with the rest of the cards.

The objective of the game is to get as many matches as you can in the shortest amount of time possible.
